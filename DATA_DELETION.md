# Data Deletion Instructions — Pingable

**Last updated:** June 14, 2026

## How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your data from Pingable, you can do so by following these steps:

### Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to **pingabledev@gmail.com** with the subject line: **"Data Deletion Request"**

Please include:
- The email address associated with your account
- Your full name

We will process your request within **30 days** and send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

### Option 2: In-App Deletion

1. Log in to your Pingable account.
2. Go to **Settings** → **Account**.
3. Click **"Delete My Account"**.
4. Confirm the deletion.

## What Data Is Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

- Your account information (name, email, profile picture)
- Your usage data and activity history
- Any content you have created within the application

## Data Retained After Deletion

Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

- Transaction records (if applicable) for tax/legal compliance
- Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you
